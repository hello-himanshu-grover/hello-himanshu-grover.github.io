About Me

I'm an Engineering Leader who thrives on creating systems that can scale seamlessly as user demand grows. My approach to engineering is deeply rooted in understanding why a problem exists — not just how to fix it.

I care about building products that make users' lives easier. That's why I focus on efficiency, clarity, and simplicity — both in design and in the way teams work.

As a mentor and technical guide, I love helping engineers connect the dots between architecture, product vision, and user experience.

While I don't claim to be an AI expert, I'm fascinated by technology fundamentals and continuously explore how AI and automation can improve developer productivity and decision-making.

When I'm not designing systems, you'll find me engaged in discussions about startups, finance, or occasionally, space — topics that fuel my curiosity and sense of scale.

"Great products are built when technology meets empathy."
